Talk2Mind · a gentle check-in

Let's check in with you.

This takes about 5 minutes. There are no right or wrong answers — it's just a moment to notice how you're actually feeling, and get a few simple suggestions if you'd find them helpful.

Everything you share stays on this page. Nothing is sent anywhere or saved.

This is a self-reflection tool, not a medical diagnosis. If you're struggling, please talk to a real person — a doctor, counselor, or someone you trust.
